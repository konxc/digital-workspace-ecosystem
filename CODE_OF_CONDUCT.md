# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Kami sebagai contributors dan maintainers berkomitmen untuk menciptakan environment yang welcoming untuk semua, regardless dari:
- Background
- Experience level
- Gender identity and expression
- Sexual orientation
- Religion
- Nationality
- Age
- Physical appearance
- Disability
- Body size
- Economic status

## Our Standards

### ✅ Behavior That We Encourage

- **Respectful**: Treat everyone with respect
- **Inclusive**: Welcome people from all backgrounds
- **Collaborative**: Work together, not compete
- **Constructive**: Give constructive feedback
- **Patient**: Be patient with newcomers
- **Professional**: Act professionally
- **Empathetic**: Understand different perspectives

### ❌ Behavior That We Don't Accept

- **Harassment**: Verbal harassment of any kind
- **Trolling**: Deliberate trolling, insulting comments
- **Discrimination**: Discriminatory jokes or language
- **Unwelcome advances**: Unwanted sexual advances
- **Personal attacks**: Personal attacks or insults
- **Publishing private info**: Publishing others' private information
- **Other conduct**: Conduct yang tidak appropriate dalam professional setting

## Scope

**Code of Conduct ini berlaku:**
- ✅ Dalam project spaces (GitHub, Discord, dll)
- ✅ Dalam public spaces (conferences, meetups)
- ✅ Dalam online spaces (social media)
- ✅ Representing project di manapun

## Reporting Violations

**Jika kamu experience atau witness behavior yang tidak sesuai:**

1. **Report ke maintainers**: Contact project maintainers
2. **Include details**: 
   - What happened
   - Where & when
   - Who was involved
3. **Confidential**: All reports will be handled confidentially

**Violations will be handled:**
- Investigated promptly
- Confidentially
- Fairly
- According to severity

2. Temporary ban
3. Permanent ban
